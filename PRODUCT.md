# Product

## Register

product

## Users

English learners using a mobile app to practice spoken scenarios in short, focused sessions. They are often mid-practice, listening, speaking, or reviewing feedback, so the interface must stay calm and readable while audio and transcript state changes quickly.

## Product Purpose

AI English Partner helps users choose a conversation scenario, complete a realtime voice practice session, receive live transcript and correction hints, then review a generated report.

## Brand Personality

Calm, focused, encouraging. The product should feel like a reliable speaking room, not a marketing page or a decorative demo.

## Anti-references

Avoid oversized voice controls, crowded connection-status copy, decorative cards, generic blue SaaS layouts, and UI that competes with subtitles during live calls.

## Design Principles

- Subtitles are the primary surface during realtime practice.
- Controls stay visible but compact, especially destructive actions.
- State is communicated with small semantic cues, not blocks of explanatory text.
- Loading and reporting states use skeletons or inline progress, not fake completed data.
- Visual hierarchy should help users speak, listen, and recover quickly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keep transcript text high contrast and large enough for mobile reading. Use reduced visual noise in the live room. Preserve clear touch targets for ending, interrupting, and starting sessions.
